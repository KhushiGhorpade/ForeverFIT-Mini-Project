Workout Plans

3-Day Full Body Workout Plan

Get started with a simple and effective workout routine to build strength and endurance.

Day 1: Full Body A

  • Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Planks: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Full Body B

  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets to failure
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 twists

Day 3: Full Body C

  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lat Pulldown: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps

3-Day Push-Pull-Legs Workout Plan

Another simple and effective workout routine for intermediate to advanced lifters.

Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulder and Triceps)

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Machine Fly: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lateral Raise: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Tricep Pushdown: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Pull (Back and Biceps)

  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 3 sets to failure
  • Lat Pulldown: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Bent-over Row: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curl: 3 sets of 20 twists
  • Dumbbell Shrugs: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 3: Legs

  • Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Hamstring Curl: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
