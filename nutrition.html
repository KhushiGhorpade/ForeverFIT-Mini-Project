Healthy Recipes

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (fresh or frozen, like bell peppers, broccoli, and carrots)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Cooked rice or noodles (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes until they are tender.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and stir to combine.
  5. Serve over cooked rice or noodles.

Egg and Spinach Breakfast Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: cheese, salsa, or hot sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, scramble the eggs over medium heat until cooked.
  2. Add the spinach and cook until wilted.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the egg and spinach mixture in the center of the tortilla, add cheese or salsa if desired, and wrap it up.
  5. Enjoy it warm!

Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and mix well.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

One-Pot Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz pasta (any type)
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: grated cheese for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine pasta, diced tomatoes (with juice), broth, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is cooked and the sauce thickens.
  3. Serve hot, topped with grated cheese if desired.

Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • Optional: 1/4 cup chocolate chips or nuts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
  3. Stir in the rolled oats and mix until combined. Add chocolate chips or nuts if using.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until lightly golden. Let cool before serving.
